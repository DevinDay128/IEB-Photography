About Me

Hello, I'm Izzy

A passionate wedding photographer dedicated to preserving the magic of your special day through timeless imagery.

Every love story is unique, and I believe your wedding photos should reflect that individuality. My approach combines documentary-style storytelling with artistic portraiture, ensuring every stolen glance, joyful tear, and heartfelt moment is captured authentically.

Based in the beautiful Hudson Valley, I've had the privilege of photographing weddings across the Northeast, from intimate garden ceremonies to grand ballroom celebrations. My goal is simple: to create images that transport you back to those precious moments, time and time again.
